RETURNS & EXCHANGES


RETURN POLICY


We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase from our store. If for any reason you're not, we're here to help. We offer a hassle-free return and exchange policy for all our products. To initiate a return or exchange, please contact us within 15 days of receiving your order.


EXCHANGE POLICY


If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or exchange. To be eligible for a return or exchange, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging. To initiate a return or exchange, please contact us within 15 days of receiving your order.
